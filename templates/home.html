Value Drivers

What usually increases house prices

  • Larger living area and efficient usable space.
  • Better condition, higher view rating, and waterfront access.
  • Modern construction or recent renovation history.
  • Balanced lot size with strong neighborhood appeal.
Buyer Perspective

How people compare properties

Buyers rarely look at one feature in isolation. They compare layout quality, bedroom-to-bathroom ratio, lot size, age of the property, and visible maintenance condition before deciding if a listed price feels justified. That is why feature-based price modeling can be so useful.
